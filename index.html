    • Pitch It!
    A cash prize or investment capital is up for grabs, so competitors are encouraged to channel their inner entrepreneurs and pitch their business ideas to a panel. The highlight event “Pitch It!”, as the name suggests; is an entry point into the prestigious world of venture capital and angel investing. Of course, you'll want to leave the finest possible impression.
    Entrepreneurial teams consisting of one to three participants each take the stage to pitch a business idea to a panel of business experts. There are two main aspects concerned to each pitch: The former being a presentation to the judges present and the latter being a QnA round, where participants must answer the questions put forth by the judges.

    Business to Brand

    "the journey revealed"

    Details AKA: Case Study competition
    Date: 2nd December 2022
    Time: 11am
    Venue: Auditorium, 2nd floor
    Eligibility: Senior & Junior
    Team Composition: 3-4 members per team
    Prize For Seniors:
    • 1st - ₹2500 + Trophy
    • 2nd - ₹1500

    For Juniors:
    1st - ₹1000
    Judging Criteria
    • Ingenuity
    • Depth of observation
    • Key aspects
    • Presentation(only applicable for round 2)
    Rules
    • The registrations will close on 28th November 2022
    • The event will consist of 2 rounds:
      • ROUND 1: Online Submission
        • The teams can choose any product of their choice
          • Once chosen please inform the coordinators
        • The presentation must include the following sub-topics:
          • Introduction to the chosen product
          • Challenges faced by the customers before the product was launched
          • How the customers demands were satisfied
          • Advantages & disadvantages of the product
          • Comparison with competitors based on advertisement strategies, revenue model etc
          • Future scope
        • The submission deadline for the presentation is set as 5 pm, 29th November 2022 (Submission link would to sent to all team leaders
        • Out of all the submissions, the best 15 will qualify for the next round (5 from junior category and 10 from senior category)
      • ROUND 2: Offline Presentation
        • Teams that qualify for this round, will present their case study on the day of the event
        • Presentation must strictly not exceed 6 minutes
        • After the presentation, the judges will ask the team a few questions related to the topic of their presentation
        • Team members are expected to contribute to the presentation equally
        • The organizing committee and the judges will make the final decisions

    Details AKA: Angel Investing
    Date: 2nd December 2022
    Time: 11am
    Venue: FiiRE Auditorium, FiiRE building
    Eligibility: Senior
    Team Composition: 1-4 members per team
    Prizes 1st - ₹8500 + Trophy
    2nd - ₹5000
    3rd - ₹2000
    Judging Criteria
    • Practical feasibility of the proposed idea
    • Scalability
    • Contribution of all members in the case of Team Participation
    • Adjourning to the criteria of presentation content namely:
      • Idea Title
      • Idea Description
      • Revenue Model
      • Challenges
      • Future Scope
    Rules
    • 2022 batch pass outs may also register
    • Registrations shall be done by 28th November 2022
    • Pitch it! is divided into 2 rounds
      • Round 1: Online submission of the ppt
        • Submission link will be provided to the team leader upon registering
        • Online submission shall be done by 5pm, 29th November 2022
        • Only best 10 teams will get an opportunity to appear for round 2
      • Round 2: Offline presentation
        • Each team will get 8 mins to pitch their idea/product and 3 minutes for Q&A session
        • Participants need to make a PowerPoint Presentation to present their idea
        • Each presentation should consist of:
          1. Idea Title
          2. Idea Description
          3. Revenue Model
          4. Challenges
          5. Future Scope
        • NOTE: Participants may choose the means of conveying as they seem fit
        • Teams may present a prototype to further convince the judges
